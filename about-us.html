How this site was made

Kittens To Go! rocks, and after looking at the mockup, I built this website from—wait for it—SCRATCH! This was certainly the most challenging lesson yet. I realize the footer differs slightly from the sample mockup, but since one of the assets given was an image that had "footer" in the filename and had the same dark gray tint and was the same aspect ratio as the gray box, I figured it had to go there. Also, notice how one of the links in the footer is different. I needed something you could click to get to the next page!
